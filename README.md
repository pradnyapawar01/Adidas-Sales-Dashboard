## ğŸ¯ Project Overview

This project involves the development of a comprehensive sales analytics dashboard for Adidas. The dashboard consolidates key sales metrics and provides interactive visualizations to analyze performance by product, retailer, region, and sales method. The goal is to offer a clear, at-a-glance view of sales health and uncover trends and patterns.

**Key Business Questions Answered:**
*   What is the total sales and profit generated?
*   Which products, regions, and retailers are the top performers?
*   How do sales trends look over time?
*   What is the performance across different sales methods (Online, In-Store, Outlet)?

## ğŸš€ Features of the Dashboard

The dashboard is designed with user-friendliness and depth of analysis in mind:

*   **High-Level KPI Overview:** Get a quick snapshot of crucial metrics like **Total Sales ($900M)** and **Total Profit**.
*   **Interactive Visualizations:**
    *   **Total Sales by Product:** A bar chart to compare sales across different Adidas product lines.
    *   **Total Sales by Month:** A line or bar chart to identify seasonal trends and growth patterns over time.
    *   **Total Sales by Retailer:** A chart comparing performance across various retail partners.
    *   **Total Sales by Sales Method:** Analysis of revenue generated through Online, In-Store, and Outlet channels.
    *   **Total Sales by Product Gender:** A breakdown of sales attributed to Men's, Women's, and Unisex products.
*   **Slicers & Filters:** Interactive filters (likely for Region, Retailer, and Month) allow users to drill down into specific segments of the data for granular analysis.
*   **Clean & Professional UI:** An Adidas-branded color scheme (black, white) for a cohesive and professional look.
